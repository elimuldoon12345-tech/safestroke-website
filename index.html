Parent & Child Program

Gentle parent-child lessons that build comfort and joy in the water through songs, toys and play. The focus: happy, calm association with the pool.

Parents join in as little swimmers explore water through engaging activities in a supportive environment. We emphasize water acclimation, sensory exploration, and creating positive water memories that last a lifetime.

Key Skills Developed:

Babies learn supported floating, gentle submersion (when ready), holding the wall, and assisted pool entries/exits. Parents learn safe handling techniques and how to support their child's aquatic development at home.

Independent Learning

Strokelet

Ages 3-12 Years

Advanced Learning & Water Safety

$40/lesson

First lesson free

Level 1 (Foundations):

Kids master breath control, floats, and the jump-resurface skill. Graduates can float 5-10 seconds and swim a few yards independently, building confidence and comfort in the water.

Level 2 (Water-Safe Swimmers):

Students refine efficiency and endurance, learning streamlined kicks, basic freestyle, and backstroke. Graduates can swim 12+ yards, float for 1+ minute, and climb out unassisted.
