About SafeStroke Swim Academy

SafeStroke Swim Academy turns caution into confidence through small groups, certified instructors, and a fundamentals-first approach.

Why Families Choose Us

Skills-First Method

Breath control and body position come first—then kicks, floats, and strokes in the right order so good habits stick.

Small Groups (3:1 Max)

More personal attention, calmer classes, and steady progress—without big-group chaos.

Certified & Safety-Led

CPR/First-Aid-certified instructors, in-water coaching, and clear deep-water rules keep kids safe.

Designed for Real Progress

Many families notice meaningful gains within 8–12 lessons (varies by age, comfort, and attendance).

Deep-Water Safety

Instructor is always in the water. Skills are taught at/along the wall; one hand stays on unless practicing a directed skill. We follow a clear Emergency Action Plan.

Progress varies by child—we meet swimmers where they are and move forward when they're ready.
