Frequently Asked Questions

Which level should I choose?

Use our Level Placement Guide above, or call us at 973-820-1153. We'll confirm placement at your first lesson and adjust if needed.

What if my child cries or is anxious?

It's normal! Our instructors are trained to handle anxiety with patience and play. Most kids warm up within a few lessons. We never force submersion.

Do parents go in the water?

Only for Droplet (3‚Äì24 months). All other programs have parents poolside for observation and support.

What should we bring?

Swimsuit, towel, and goggles (optional for beginners). Children not potty-trained need swim diapers. We provide kickboards and other equipment.

Make-ups & cancellations?

We offer make-up lessons with 24-hour notice. See our full policy at booking or contact us for details.

Hygiene & safety?

All instructors are certified. We maintain small ratios (3-4:1 for most classes), regular water testing, and follow strict safety protocols.

How fast will my child progress?

Most families see meaningful progress in 8‚Äì12 lessons. Every child is unique‚Äîconsistency and comfort matter more than speed.
