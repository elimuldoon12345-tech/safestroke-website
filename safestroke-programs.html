Programs & Curriculum

Our swim classes at SafeStroke Swim Academy focus on steady, skill-driven progress — blending personalized instruction with small group dynamics to help each swimmer grow in confidence, safety, and technique week after week.

Strokelets

Ages 3-12 years

Bubble Buddies

(Level 1)

3:1 Swimmer to Instructor

An introduction to water safety and comfort for beginners. Kids learn breath control, supported floats, and basic water skills in a fun, supportive setting—building confidence for independent swimming.

Book Lessons

Stroke Starters

(Level 2)

3:1 Swimmer to Instructor

Designed for kids ready to build independence through skills. Focuses on safe pool entry, breath control, floating, kicking, and intro to doggy paddle and freestyle — progressing towards a safe, independent swimmer.

For Children Under 3 years

Water Acclimation Classes

Droplet

Ages 3-24 months (Parent & Child) | 8:1 Ratio

Safety Skills: Safe submersion (when ready), holding the wall, assisted entries/exits.

Foundations: Supported floating/kicking, blowing bubbles, face in water.

Goals: Build water comfort & trust, teach parents safe handling.

Book Lessons

Splashlet

Ages 2-3 years | 4:1 Ratio

Safety Skills: Submersion & breath control, short propulsion to safety.

Skill Foundations: Supported back/front floats, guided movement.

Goals: Foster water comfort, build instructor trust, foundational for independence.
