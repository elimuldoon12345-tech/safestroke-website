Water Acclimation Classes

Droplet

Ages 3-24 months (Parent & Child) | 8:1 Ratio

Safety Skills: Safe submersion (when ready), holding the wall, assisted entries/exits.

Foundations: Supported floating/kicking, blowing bubbles, face in water.

Goals: Build water comfort & trust, teach parents safe handling.

Book Lessons

Splashlet

Ages 2-3 years | 4:1 Ratio

Safety Skills: Submersion & breath control, short propulsion to safety.

Skill Foundations: Supported back/front floats, guided movement.

Goals: Foster water comfort, build instructor trust, foundational for independence.
