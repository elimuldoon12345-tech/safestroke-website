Level Placement Guide

Not sure where to start? Use this quick guide.

Under 2 years or needs a parent in water → Droplet

Age 2–3, not ready without parent → Splashlet

Age 3+ and water-shy or learning basics → Strokelet L1

Age 3+ and comfortable face-in, ready for skills & short swims → Strokelet L2

Note: We confirm placement at your first lesson and adjust as needed.

Level 1

Strokelet L1 (Bubble Buddies)

Ages 3–12 | 3:1 Swimmer-to-Instructor

Best for: Beginners or water-shy

You'll see them learn:

  • Breath control & calm water comfort
  • Supported back/front floats; supported kicks
  • Safe wall holds & gentle submersion when ready

Goal in ~8–12 lessons: short, calm independent swims; safe entries and returns to wall

Level 2

Strokelet L2 (Stroke Starters)

Ages 3–12 | 3:1 Swimmer-to-Instructor

Best for: Comfortable in water & skill-ready

You'll see them learn:

  • Controlled breath, floating, kicking with body line
  • Streamlined push-offs; intro to front crawl arms
  • Rollover breathing & safe returns to wall

Goal in ~8–12 lessons: consistent short independent swims; early stroke patterns

L1 vs L2: What's the difference?

Comfort: L1 builds calm water comfort; L2 builds skillful independence
Skills: L1 = supported floats & kicks; L2 = streamline, arms, rollover breathing
Outcome: L1 = first short swims; L2 = consistent short swims with form

Frequently Asked Questions

Which level should I choose?

Use our Level Placement Guide above, or call us at 973-820-1153. We'll confirm placement at your first lesson.

What if my child cries or is anxious?

It's normal! Our instructors handle anxiety with patience and play. Most kids warm up within a few lessons.

Do parents go in the water?

Only for Droplet (3–24 months). All other programs have parents poolside for observation and support.

What should we bring?

Swimsuit, towel, and goggles (optional for beginners). Children not potty-trained need swim diapers.

Make-ups & cancellations?

We offer make-up lessons with 24-hour notice. See our full policy at booking.

Hygiene & safety?

All instructors are certified. We maintain small ratios and follow strict safety protocols.

How fast will my child progress?

Most families see meaningful progress in 8–12 lessons. Every child is unique—consistency matters more than speed.
